About Me.



I am currently a visiting researcher at the World Well-Being Project in the University of Pennsylvania, working with Prof. Lyle Ungar. I am a PhD student at NTU Singapore working with Prof. Weisi Lin in the Multimedia Graduate Lab.

I joined NTU in August 2013. Prior to joining NTU, I was an undergraduate student at Birla Institute of Technology and Sciences (BITS) - Pilani , India, from where I graduated in 2013 with a B.E.(Hons) in Computer Science.

My research focuses on modelling the influence of human factors, such as personality and culture, on users' experience of affect, perception of quality and enjoyment in multimedia.
