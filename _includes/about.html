About Me.



I am a research scholar at the World Well-Being Project in the University of Pennsylvania, working with Prof. Lyle Ungar. Before this, I was a PhD student at NTU Singapore working with Prof. Weisi Lin in the Multimedia Graduate Lab.

I joined NTU in August 2013. Prior to joining NTU, I was an undergraduate student at Birla Institute of Technology and Sciences (BITS) - Pilani , India, from where I graduated in 2013 with a B.E.(Hons) in Computer Science.

My research focuses on modelling user factors, such as personality and culture, based on multimodal data generated by users on social media.
